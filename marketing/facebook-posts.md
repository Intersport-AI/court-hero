## Posting Guidelines

- **Join the group first**, engage normally for a few days before posting
- **Never post the same thing in multiple groups on the same day**
- **Respond to every comment** — this is community building
- **Don't be defensive** if someone mentions a competitor — acknowledge them and highlight what makes Court Hero different (no app download, instant setup)
- **Best times to post:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 AM or 6-8 PM local time
- **Target groups:** Search Facebook for "pickleball [city name]" — there are 200+ active groups
